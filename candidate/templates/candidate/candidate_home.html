What is an Applicant?

An Applicant is one of the two account types that can apply to job listings yo can find job listings in the 'Job Board', once you apply the application will be sent to the Employer account where they can review and approve your application. If an application is approved the applicant can choose to make the application "Dormant". When an application is made dormant it puts the position on hold, this allows for applications to never expire.
